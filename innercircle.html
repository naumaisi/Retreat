Aurelia Virelli

Co-Founder • Spiritual Visionary & Healer Aurelia’s journey began as a seeker — a soul drawn to the stillness of nature and the wisdom of ancient rituals. In Bali, she found her purpose in bridging the worlds of spirit and earth through ceremony, mindfulness, and community. Her intuitive guidance and radiant calm have become the heartbeat of InnerAcademy. Aurelia leads moon gatherings, feminine healing circles, and integration workshops that help guests reconnect with their essence and inner peace.


Kael Virelli

Co-Founder • Mindfulness Mentor & Earth Steward Grounded yet deeply introspective, Kael brings balance to the InnerAcademy through his passion for conscious living and holistic philosophy. Formerly a wellness architect and meditation teacher, he now devotes his life to creating environments where others can thrive naturally. Kael oversees sustainability projects, teaches breathwork and grounding practices, and ensures that every retreat remains rooted in respect for nature and intentional living.


Leandro “Leo” Amai

Holistic Chef & Culinary Alchemist Leo’s cuisine is an art form — vibrant, soulful, and alive. Born in Bahia, Brazil, and trained in holistic nutrition and Ayurvedic cooking, he believes food is the most sacred form of medicine. Each meal he creates nourishes both body and spirit, weaving together local ingredients, intuition, and gratitude. His kitchen is a sanctuary where flavors become pathways to healing.


Sahana Devi

Yoga & Movement Guide With a presence that feels both grounding and ethereal, Sahana leads her students into alignment with their breath, body, and awareness. Having trained in Rishikesh and traveled through Asia studying meditation and somatic healing, she blends traditional yoga with intuitive movement. Her classes invite participants to listen deeply — to find softness in strength and stillness in motion.
