About InnerAcademy

InnerAcademy was founded by Aurelia & Kael Virelli, a visionary couple who met on the lush shores of Bali and instantly felt the magic of its vibrant, natural community. Surrounded by serene jungles, whispering rice terraces, and the calming rhythm of ocean waves, they realized that a conscious, supportive community rooted in nature could unlock profound spiritual growth and elevate life experiences. Inspired, they dedicated themselves to creating a sanctuary where people from all corners of the globe could reconnect—with themselves, with others, and with the natural world. Today, InnerAcademy offers immersive retreats that go beyond traditional wellness experiences. Guests can stay in charming shared or private cottages designed to blend seamlessly with the environment, waking up to the sound of birdsong and gentle breezes. Every day is an invitation to nourish the body, mind, and spirit: enjoy high-nutrition meals crafted by holistic chefs, relax with restorative spa treatments, or engage in mindful bodywork sessions. Spiritual practices such as Kambo, Hapé, Ayahuasca, and guided mushroom ceremonies are offered in a safe, professional environment to support deep transformation. To ensure a focused and supportive atmosphere, retreats are held separately for male and female participants, allowing everyone to fully immerse themselves in their personal journey. Beyond the core offerings, guests can explore additional activities such as guided jungle walks, meditation by tranquil waterfalls, art therapy, sound healing, drumming circles, and yoga classes tailored for all levels. There are also specialized workshops with yoga instructors and holistic nutrition experts, so participants can take home practical knowledge to continue their healing journey long after the retreat ends. As part of their mission to expand access to transformative experiences, Aurelia & Kael host a scholarship lottery after every retreat, giving select individuals the opportunity to join and experience life-changing growth. InnerAcademy is more than a retreat—it is a sanctuary of calm and inspiration, a vibrant community that celebrates holistic living, and a transformative journey where every moment is designed to awaken your highest potential.
